David Hartman

Artist / Cartoonist / Character Design

Trick or Treat Studios is thrilled to be the exclusive Halloween Mask Maker for the artwork of David Hartman and his characters from Side Show Monkey.

David mastered his craft at the Columbus College of Art and Design and went on to do animation at Sony Pictures and Walt Disney's Television divisions.

Although achieving fame for his Directorial work on Disney's My Friends Tigger and Pooh, we love David most for his Side Show Monkey Characters and the amazing art work he did for Rob Zombie's American Witch and Lords of Salem animated videos.

As a character designer, David earned accolades for his unique designs featured in MTV's Spiderman.

David's other credits include visual effects supervisor for the cult film "Bubba Ho-Tep" and production consultant for Showtime's "Masters of Horror" series.

In addition to his television animation work, David is an award winning illustrator whose artwork graces numerous comic books, magazines and music albums covers."

David is also the founder and owner of http://www.sideshowmonkey.com.
