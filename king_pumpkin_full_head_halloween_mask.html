King Pumpkin Halloween Mask$49.99

Sculpted by Justin Mabry

Every great Halloween Mask collection needs a Scary Pumpkin Mask.  With that in mind, our Art Director, Justin Mabry, decided to take up the task himself.  But Justin didn’t just make any Pumpkin Mask, no, he made the King of all Pumpkin Halloween Masks, King Pumpkin.

Molded from an actual rotting Pumpkin that Justin found in his backyard, this is the first Pumpkin Mask ever cast from an actual Pumpkin.

The front of the mask has a very scary Nightmare Before Christmas, one of our favorite movies, look and feel to it, and the back is covered in rotting pumpkin flesh, making this mask appropriately titled King Pumpkin, the true King among Scary Pumpkin Halloween Masks.
